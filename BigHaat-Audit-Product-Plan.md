- NPOP-certified organic fertilizers
- Vermicompost (packaged, branded)

- Pheromone traps & lures (crop-specific: fruit fly, stem borer, etc.)

- Biological pest control agents — Trichoderma, Beauveria bassiana, Metarhizium, Verticillium
- Organic growth promoters / bio-stimulants
- Humic acid & fulvic acid (liquid & granule)

### C. Farm Tools & Implements (Expanded)
- Power weeders (petrol)
- Walking / mini tractors

- Manual & motorized harvesting tools (sickles, reapers)
- Brush cutters (petrol)

- Hand pruners, grafting knives, budding tools

### D. Hydroponics & Soilless Growing (Inputs Only)

### E. Post-Harvest & Storage

- Post-harvest fungicide dips & wax coatings (for fruits)

- Packaging twine & binding materials

### F. Animal Husbandry (Expanded)
- Poultry feed — broiler starter, grower, finisher; layer feed
- Cattle feed concentrates & mineral mixtures
- Goat & sheep feed
- Pig feed
- Aquaculture feed (fish pellets — floating & sinking)
- Silage additives (for fodder preservation)
- Veterinary OTC products — dewormers, vitamin supplements, calcium bolus

- Fodder seeds — Napier, Sorghum, Berseem, Lucerne

### G. Soil Health Products
- Soil test kits (chemical colour-comparison type — NPK, pH, EC)
- Gypsum (for sodic soil correction)
- Agricultural lime / dolomite
- Elemental sulfur (for alkaline soil)
- Bio-inoculants — Rhizobium, PSB (Phosphate Solubilizing Bacteria), Azospirillum, Azotobacter, KMB
- Mycorrhizal fungi inoculants
- Soil conditioners / wetting agents

### H. Home & Kitchen Gardening
- Balcony / terrace gardening starter kits
- Raised bed planters (wooden, galvanized iron)
- Potting mix / ready garden soil
- Compost for home gardens (small packs)
- Organic plant nutrients (liquid, for home use)
- Garden hand tools — trowels, cultivators, pruners, watering cans
- Seed starter / germination kits (for home)
- Vegetable seeds for kitchen garden (small packs — tomato, chilli, coriander, methi, spinach)
- Plant supports — bamboo stakes, coir rope, trellises

### I. Seeds (Expanded)
- Medicinal & aromatic plant seeds (Ashwagandha, Tulsi, Aloe Vera, Lemongrass)
- Fodder crop seeds (Napier grass, Berseem, Cowpea, Sorghum)

- Sprout seeds (Fenugreek, Moong for home sprouting)
- Open-pollinated variety (OPV) seeds for organic farmers

## 3. Prioritized Addition Plan

### Phase 1 — Quick Wins (0–3 months)
*Easy to source, high farmer demand*

1. **Drip & Sprinkler Irrigation Kits** — Govt. subsidy-backed, all competitors have it, high search volume
2. **Vermicompost, Neem Cake, Neem Oil** — Organic farming growing fast, easy to onboard regional brands
3. **Pheromone & Sticky Traps** — Low cost, high demand, IPM adoption rising
4. **Hermetic Grain Storage Bags** — Massive rural need, very few online sellers
5. **Power Weeders** — Labour shortage makes this a top priority for farmers
6. **Poultry & Cattle Feed (expanded SKUs)** — Already in catalogue, just needs depth
7. **Cocopeat & Growing Media** — Cheap to ship, high margin, urban + rural demand
8. **Kitchen Garden Seed Packs** — Small packs, urban consumer, easy to list

### Phase 2 — Expansion (3–6 months)
*Needs supplier tie-ups or licensing*

9. **Bio-inoculants (Rhizobium, PSB, Azospirillum)** — Organic input, massive crop coverage
10. **Biological Pest Control Agents** — Trichoderma, Beauveria: replacing chemical pesticides
11. **Fodder Seeds** — Goes with expanded animal husbandry
12. **Soil Correction Products** — Gypsum, lime, sulfur: large volume, low margin but sticky
13. **Veterinary OTC Products** — Dewormers, calcium bolus, vitamin supplements
14. **Post-harvest Fungicide & Wax Coatings** — For fruit farmers (mango, banana, citrus)
15. **Hydroponic Nutrient Solutions** — For commercial growers, schools, urban farms
16. **Raised Bed Planters & Garden Tools** — Home gardening category launch

### Phase 3 — Differentiation (6–12 months)
*New segments, wider catalogue*

17. **Medicinal & Aromatic Plant Seeds** — Niche but high-value (Ashwagandha, Lemongrass)
18. **Aquaculture Feed & Supplements** — Fish & shrimp farming inputs
19. **Silage Additives** — Dairy farmer segment
20. **Green Manure / Cover Crop Seeds** — Soil health trend
21. **OPV Seeds for Organic Farmers** — Differentiated from hybrid seed competitors
